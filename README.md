# Crime Against Women Analysis in India

## Project Overview
This project analyzes crime against women trends in India using Python, Pandas, Seaborn, and Matplotlib.

The analysis focuses on identifying:
- States with highest and lowest crime cases
- Yearly crime trends
- Most common crime categories
- Growth of assault on minors over time
- Relationship between domestic violence and assault on women
- Contribution of top states to total crime cases

---

Dataset used:

## Key Insights

### Highest Crime States
- Uttar Pradesh reported the highest number of crime cases.
- Madhya Pradesh and West Bengal also showed very high crime records.

### Lowest Crime States
- Lakshadweep recorded the lowest crime cases.
- Smaller northeastern states generally showed lower crime totals.

### Crime Trends
- Crime cases steadily increased from 2001 to 2010.
- Sharp growth was observed after 2012.
- Crime levels remained high with fluctuations after 2015.

### Crime Categories
- Domestic Violence contributed the largest share of total crimes.
- Women Trafficking recorded the lowest contribution.

### Assault on Minors
- Cases remained stable initially.
- Strong spike observed between 2013–2016.
- Significant decline occurred after 2016.

### Correlation Analysis
- Domestic Violence and Assault on Women showed a positive correlation of 0.63.
- This suggests a moderate relationship between both crime categories.

### State Contribution
- Top 10 states contributed a major share of total crime cases in India.
